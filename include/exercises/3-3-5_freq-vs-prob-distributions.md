Solution
========

Histograms are shown, given the random variable blood pressure is defined over a continuous interval. It is true that histrograms should not be represented with gaps between bars.

Histograms show frequency distribution: occurrences observed from experimental data.

The curves model the probability of observing blood pressure within the whole interval
