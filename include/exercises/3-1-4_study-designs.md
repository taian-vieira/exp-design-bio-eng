Solution
========

The figure illustrates an interventional longitudinal study design. However, there is no information indicating whether subjects were randomized between groups. Therefore, the study cannot be classified as a randomized controlled trial (RCT).

The control group did not receive any treatment intervention and therefore corresponds to a no-treatment control group.

Blinding was not possible because subjects undergoing the diet intervention necessarily knew that their diet had changed.
