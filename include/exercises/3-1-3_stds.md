Question
========
*The mean values for BMI and for the waist circumference can be defined directly by inspecting @fig-3-1-4. **What are the standard deviations for BMI and waist circumference?***

Answerlist
----------
* 20.3 kg/m^2^, 76.3 cm
* 5.1 kg/m^2^, 14.0 cm
* 30.5 kg/m^2^, 104.3 cm
* None of the above

Solution
========

There are different possibilities for computing the standard deviations from @fig-3-1-4.

The simplest approach would be subtracting the value under the Mean ($\mu$) column from those in te $+1\sigma$ column, yielding:

- $5.1 \text{ kg/m}^2$ for BMI
- $14.0 \text{ cm}$ for waist circumference
