Question
========

*How can the probability of a Type I error be decreased within the framework of hypothesis testing?*

Answerlist
----------
* increasing sample size
* increasing $\beta$
* decreasing $\beta$
* increasing $\alpha$
* decreasing $\alpha$


Solution
========

Since $\alpha$ represents the probability of a Type I error, decreasing $\alpha$ directly reduces the probability of making such an error.

However, for a fixed sample size, decreasing $\alpha$ generally increases the probability of a Type II error ($\beta$).
