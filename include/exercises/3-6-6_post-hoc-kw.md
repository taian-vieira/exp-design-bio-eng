Question
========

*Which of the post-hoc analysis listed below would be indicated for the pairwise comprisons?*

---

Answerlist
----------
* Fisher LSD
* Bonferroni method
* Tukey--Kramer
* Dunnett test
* none of them

Solution
========

Except for the Bonferroni correction, the other post-hoc methods rely on the assumption that the distribution of the errors is Normal.
